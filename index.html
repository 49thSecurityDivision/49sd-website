49th Security Division

About

We are a Computer Security and Ethical Hacking club based out of UNC Charlotte. We were founded in 2009 and continue to compete in premier security competitions and attend top notch hacker and cybersecurity conferences.

How to Join

Simply write us an Email and then join us on Slack to become a member of the club.

Doing so will allow you to:

  • Participate in Meetings
  • Practice for competitions
  • Attend competition practices(For competitions that do not require membership)
  • Membership guidelines are mentioned in the Slack.
  • Attend lab sessions
